our story

Immigrating To the United States at age 16 from Ayutla Jalisco Mexico, Gorgonio Anaya had dreams of making a better life for him and his family. He began his journey picking fruits and vegetables in the fields of Milpitas, CA going door to door while trying to make a living.

Eventually, after years of hard work, he became an entrepreneur and Established his own furniture company, Muebleria Ayutla in 1985 and has been family owned and operated in the same location for over 30 years
